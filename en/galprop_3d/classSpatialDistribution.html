Access the distribution in an azimuthally averaged fashion.

Default operator takes the azimuthal average of the 3D distribution. Override for 2D distributions and distributions with analytic form for the azimuthal average.

Parameters
ris the radial part of cylindrical coordinates centered on the GC
zis the distance above the galactic plane, positive z in the northern hemisphere

The units for the distances are in kpc.
